💡 Thumbnail Tips That Get Clicks
😱
Faces with extreme emotions (shock, fear, joy) get 2-3x more clicks than no-face thumbnails
🔢
Numbers in thumbnails ("I got 1,000,000 kills") trigger curiosity — always use them if possible

📏
Keep text to 3-4 words max. Big, bold, readable at thumbnail size (small on mobile)
🎯
Show the result, not the process. Viewers click to see the outcome, not what you're doing
